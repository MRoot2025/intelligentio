The High Cost of Slow Onboarding

The average company loses 3 full days of productivity per new hire due to manual IT setup. Manual processes also introduce security risks: forgotten permission revocations, orphaned accounts, and inconsistent security policy application. Your IT team burns out on repetitive tasks that should be automated, and every manual step is an opportunity for error that could lead to a compliance violation or data breach.

The Intelligent iT 5-Minute Onboarding

Our process is simple: HR enters a new hire in your HRIS, our AI automatically provisions every account, permission, and device profile based on their role, and the new employee receives a welcome email with everything they need to be productive from minute one. What used to take days now takes under 5 minutes, with zero manual IT intervention and zero security gaps.

The AI Advantage: From Manual to Automated

We connect directly to your HRIS—Rippling, Gusto, or BambooHR. When your HR team marks a candidate as hired, it triggers a secure, role-based workflow in our platform. This workflow automatically creates the user in Azure Active Directory, assigns them to the correct security groups, provisions a Microsoft 365 license, deploys pre-configured software via Intune or Jamf, enrolls them in security awareness training, and sends a personalized welcome email. Offboarding is equally automated: a single trigger revokes all access, archives data, wipes corporate information from devices, and generates a complete compliance audit trail. Every action is logged, auditable, and executed without human error.
