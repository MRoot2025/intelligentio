What Information We Collect and Why

We collect only the data necessary to deliver and secure our services. This includes user credentials for service provisioning and authentication, network traffic metadata for security monitoring and threat detection, endpoint telemetry for performance optimization and predictive maintenance, and support interaction records for service quality improvement. Each data type has a specific, documented business purpose, and we retain data only as long as required to fulfill that purpose.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for service delivery, security monitoring, and support improvement. Client data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Any data used to improve our AI models is first aggregated and anonymized, ensuring no individual client data can be identified or reconstructed. Our automated data handling policies enforce these commitments continuously and without exception.
