Midtown Manhattan: Where It All Started

In 2000, Manuel Ruiz started Intelligent iT from a small office in Midtown Manhattan with a simple belief: businesses deserve IT that actually works. No jargon. No excuses. Just results.

New York City doesn't tolerate mediocrity. When a Wall Street firm's network goes down, minutes cost millions. When a healthcare practice loses access to patient records, lives are at stake. We were forged in that pressure — and it shaped everything about how we operate today.

25 years later, we're still headquartered in Midtown, still founder-led, and still obsessed with the same thing: keeping businesses running.

NYC is the #2 tech hub in the United States, home to over 25,000 tech companies and 400,000 tech workers.
