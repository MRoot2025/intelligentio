The AI Advantage: From Q&A to Proactive Workflows

Our AI Knowledgebase does more than just retrieve information; it actively improves your business operations. If three employees ask a similar question that is not in your documentation, the system automatically flags this as a knowledge gap and assigns a task to the relevant department head to create a new SOP.
